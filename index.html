ABOUT US

Every college strives to create an atmosphere on it's campus which promotes academic as well as co-curricular excellence.

Shutterbugs, the Photography Club of MITAOE, makes this come true. This club focuses on enriching students' interest in photography and teaches how to convert moments into long-lasting memories.

This club conducts different activities for it's members round the year and some activities at the college and intercollegiate level as well. There are events such as Photowalk around different places of the city, Photography Exhibitions on various genres, Training sessions for the club members. This club also covers various major events of the college including College Fests and plenty of other events.
